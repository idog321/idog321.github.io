Capabilities

Built for the field

Everything you need to collect spatial data, navigate terrain, and manage geographic information — designed for professionals who work outdoors.

Real Coordinate Systems

Work in any projection. Over 6,000 EPSG codes supported natively, powered by the PROJ library and NGA specifications. Enter and display coordinates in DD, DMS, DDM, or UTM.

Offline-First

Cache map tiles and elevation data for offline use. Your fieldwork does not depend on cell service. Everything works in airplane mode, deep in the backcountry.

Elevation Data

Global 30-metre elevation from the Copernicus GLO-30 DEM, cached on your device. Get elevation for any point without an internet connection or API key.

Feature Collection

Drop points, draw lines and polygons directly on the map. Attach attributes, organize by layer, and manage your spatial data with a clean, focused interface.

Routing & Navigation

Plan routes with turn-by-turn directions using Apple Maps infrastructure. Multiple input methods: search, drop pins, or use your current location as a waypoint.

Privacy by Design

No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. Your data lives on your device and syncs through your personal iCloud. We never see it, and we never will.

GIS without compromise

Professional tools, personal privacy, one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no data harvesting, no surprises.
