Common Questions

Does TopoKit work offline?

Yes. Map tiles and elevation data are cached on your device as you browse. Once cached, you can work without an internet connection. For areas you plan to visit, zoom around them while connected to pre-cache the data.

How accurate is the elevation data?

TopoKit uses the Copernicus GLO-30 Digital Elevation Model, which has a horizontal resolution of approximately 30 metres. This is useful for terrain awareness, route planning, and general fieldwork, but should not be treated as survey-grade measurement.

Can I export my data?

Export functionality is on the roadmap. Stay tuned for updates through the App Store release notes.

Does my data sync across devices?

If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac automatically through your personal iCloud account. No third-party servers are involved.

What coordinate systems are supported?

TopoKit supports over 6,000 EPSG coordinate reference systems through the PROJ library and NGA specifications. You can enter and display coordinates in decimal degrees, degrees-minutes-seconds, degrees-decimal minutes, or UTM.

Is there a subscription?

No. TopoKit is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or recurring fees.
